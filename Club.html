Club Lounge Access

Considered a hotel within The Ritz-Carlton, Toronto, the luxurious guest rooms and suites cater to the elevated experience within the Club Lounge. Outside of the exclusive club-level rooms, guests will enjoy the heated indoor saltwater pool, Spa myBlend, and multiple dining options. All Club Level rooms feature a high-floor location with spectacular views of the Toronto skyline.
